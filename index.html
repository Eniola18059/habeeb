Who We Are

A community built to bridge the gap

WILLNET was created to provide women with practical opportunities to grow their leadership skills while building meaningful connections with other women on similar journeys.

Too often, women are told to "be confident" or "step up," yet they are rarely given the tools, mentorship, or supportive environments needed to actually develop those capabilities.

At WILLNET, leadership is not about titles — it is about influence, growth, and impact. We believe it can be learned, practiced, and developed in community.

Meet the Founder

The vision behind WILLNET

WILLNET was founded by Aisha Umar, a leader, strategist, and community builder deeply committed to empowering women and supporting leadership development.

With more than a decade of experience across nonprofit, business, and community sectors, Aisha has led initiatives in program development, leadership training, and social impact.

She observed a common challenge: many talented women possess incredible potential but lack access to environments where they can confidently develop their leadership. WILLNET bridges that gap.

"Leadership is not limited to titles — it is about influence, growth, and the courage to step forward."
