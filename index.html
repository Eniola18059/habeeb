Who We Are

A community built to bridge the gap

WILLNET was created to provide women with practical opportunities to grow their leadership skills while building meaningful connections with other women on similar journeys.

Too often, women are told to "be confident" or "step up," yet they are rarely given the tools, mentorship, or supportive environments needed to actually develop those capabilities.

At WILLNET, leadership is not about titles — it is about influence, growth, and impact. We believe it can be learned, practiced, and developed in community.

Meet the Founder

The vision behind WILLNET

WILLNET was founded by Aisha Umar, a leader, strategist, and community builder deeply committed to empowering women and supporting leadership development.

With more than a decade of experience across nonprofit, business, and community sectors, Aisha has led initiatives in program development, leadership training, and social impact.

She observed a common challenge: many talented women possess incredible potential but lack access to environments where they can confidently develop their leadership. WILLNET bridges that gap.

"Leadership is not limited to titles — it is about influence, growth, and the courage to step forward."

Got Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? We're always happy to help — reach out directly and a member of our team will get back to you.

 hello@willnet.org
WILLNET (Women in Learning & Leadership Network) is a leadership and learning community built for women and girls who want to grow with clarity, confidence, and purpose. Whether you're early in your career, navigating a leadership role, building a business, or mentoring the next generation — WILLNET has a place for you.
Yes — completely free, no credit card required. Free members get access to selected community events, leadership newsletters and resources, invitations to open workshops, access to the WILLNET podcast community, and networking opportunities. It's the perfect starting point for your leadership journey.
The Leadership Circle is our premium tier for women committed to intentional growth. On top of everything in the Free Membership, you get access to Mentorship Circles, monthly leadership learning sessions, exclusive leadership development resources, priority event invitations, member-only discussions, and opportunities to mentor younger women in the community.
Girls Corner is WILLNET's youth initiative designed specifically for teenage girls. It focuses on confidence building, leadership development, communication and public speaking, identity and purpose, and mentorship. It's tailored for girls roughly between the ages of 13–19, though we're flexible — reach out if you have questions about eligibility.
Mentorship Circles are small, curated groups of women who meet regularly — typically virtually or in-person depending on your location. Each circle focuses on accountability, shared leadership challenges, peer support, and guided reflection. Groups are intentionally small to ensure every voice is heard. This feature is available to Leadership Circle members.
WILLNET is built to be accessible globally. Most programs and events are offered online so women can participate from anywhere in the world. We also host in-person community gatherings and networking events periodically. Subscribe to updates to stay informed about events near you.
We welcome organizations that believe in the power of women's leadership development. Partnership and sponsorship opportunities include co-hosting events, sponsoring programs and initiatives, corporate membership packages, and more. To explore a partnership, reach out to us at partnerships@willnet.org and our team will be in touch.
