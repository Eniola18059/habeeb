Our Origin

How WILLNET came to be

WILLNET (Women in Learning & Leadership Network) was created to provide women with practical opportunities to grow their leadership skills while building meaningful connections with other women on similar journeys.

Too often, women are told to simply "be confident" or "step up," yet they are rarely given the tools, mentorship, or supportive environments needed to actually develop those leadership capabilities.

"We bridge the gap between potential and possibility — creating the environments that talented women have always deserved."

At WILLNET, leadership is not about titles. It is about influence, growth, and the courage to impact your world — whether that's your workplace, your family, your community, or beyond. We believe it can be learned, practiced, and developed in community.

This is not just a network. It is a space where women learn together, support one another, and rise together.

What Drives Us

Our mission & vision

🎯
Mission

Empowering women through education, mentorship, and community

WILLNET exists to empower women and girls through leadership education, mentorship, and community support that helps them grow personally, professionally, and socially. We provide the tools, connections, and environments that enable every woman to step into her full leadership potential.

🌍
Vision

A global community of confident, capable women leaders

To cultivate a global community of confident, capable women leaders who are equipped to influence their industries, strengthen their communities, and inspire the next generation. We envision a world where every woman has access to the growth, mentorship, and opportunity she needs to lead.

The person behind the mission

Aisha Umar — leader, strategist, community builder

WILLNET was founded by Aisha Umar, a leader deeply committed to empowering women and supporting leadership development at every stage of life.

With more than a decade of experience across nonprofit, business, and community sectors, Aisha has worked extensively in program development, leadership training, and social impact initiatives. She has built, led, and supported organizations that have helped hundreds of individuals unlock their potential.

Throughout her career, Aisha observed a consistent, disheartening pattern: talented women — brilliant, capable, ready — holding back. Not because of lack of ambition, but because the infrastructure to support their growth simply wasn't there. No mentors who looked like them. No safe spaces to practice leadership. No communities that understood their specific journey.

WILLNET was created to change that.

"Leadership is not limited to titles — it is about influence, growth, and the courage to step forward. Every woman who steps up creates permission for the next one to follow."

— Aisha Umar, Founder of WILLNET

Our Journey

Building something that lasts

2014

The Idea is Born

After years of observing the leadership gap that talented women faced, the vision for WILLNET begins to take shape.

2017

First Community Events

WILLNET hosts its inaugural community gatherings, bringing together women for authentic connection and learning.

2019

Mentorship Circles Launch

The flagship Mentorship Circles program is established, providing structured peer-to-peer support and guided leadership reflection.

2021

Girls Corner Initiative

WILLNET expands its mission to reach teenage girls through Girls Corner, building the next generation of confident leaders early.

2023

The WILLNET Podcast

Honest conversations about leadership, career, and life go global — now reaching women worldwide through the WILLNET Podcast.

Now

Growing Global

WILLNET is expanding its reach, refining its programs, and building towards a global leadership network with conferences, fellowships, and publications.
