Meet the Host
Aisha Umar — host, founder, leader
Founder of WILLNET · Host of the WILLNET Podcast

The WILLNET Podcast is hosted by Aisha Umar, the founder of WILLNET and a leader with over a decade of experience in nonprofit, business, and community development.

Aisha brings to every episode the same qualities that define WILLNET itself: honesty, depth, and a genuine belief in what women are capable of when given the right environment. She asks the questions most interviewers avoid — and creates a space where guests share with unusual candour.

Each conversation is grounded in real experience, designed to move you — not just inspire you — and leave you with something you can actually use.

"I started this podcast because I was tired of leadership content that sounded good but felt hollow. These conversations are for women who are ready to do the real work."
